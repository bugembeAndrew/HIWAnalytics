Sending a recommendation or advice to a patient whose spouse or baby are not yet tested.

To send a recommendation or advice to an ART Patient registered in our program. Simply follow these steps below:-

  1. Copy the patient's telephone number from either Patients' details whose babies are not tested or Patients' details whose spouses are not tested.
  2. Add '+' at the start of the number copied.(required format is +256XXXXXXXXX)
  3. Enter best suited recommendation for the given patient in the space provided in the body field.
  4. Finally click send message button
